Peanut Butter Cookies

Home baked peanut butter cookies

These peanut butter cookies are soft, rich, and packed with deep, nutty flavor. Each bite delivers the perfect balance of sweet and salty, with a melt-in-your-mouth texture that’s both tender and chewy. The classic crisscross top gives them a nostalgic touch, but it’s the bold peanut butter taste that makes them unforgettable. Simple to make and impossible to resist, they’re the ultimate comfort cookie.

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients
  2. form into multiple balls
  3. Cook in the oven
