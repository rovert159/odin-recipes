Goulash

Homemade goulash in under 30 minutes

With its rich tomato sauce, tender pasta, and flavorful ground beef, this one-pot dish is perfect for busy nights when you crave something hearty and filling. The simple, familiar flavors come together effortlessly, making every bite a cozy reminder of home. Whether it's the depth of the tomatoes or the creamy finish of melted cheese, American goulash is the kind of meal that warms you from the inside out, leaving you ready for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Noodles(preferably macaroni)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef and onion together.
  2. Add in the tomato sauce and Worcestershire sauce.
  3. Add in the noodles and let cook until noodles are cooked through.
  4. While noodles are cooking add and taste for salt until the right level of saltiness.
  5. Stir in shredded cheese and dig in!
